Privacy Policy for DoggyDaily

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use DoggyDaily, the daily photo capture app for your beloved dogs.

Information We Collect

Photos and Media

DoggyDaily allows you to capture one photo of your dog per day. These photos are:

  • Stored locally on your device
  • Backed up to your personal iCloud account (if you have iCloud backup enabled)
  • Never transmitted to our servers or any third-party services
  • Completely private and only accessible by you

iCloud Data

When you use DoggyDaily with iCloud enabled:

  • Your dog photos and app data are stored in your personal iCloud account
  • This data syncs across your devices signed into the same iCloud account
  • We do not have access to your iCloud data
  • Data backup and sync are handled entirely by Apple's iCloud service

How We Use Your Information

We do not collect, use, or have access to any of your personal information or your dog photos. All data remains on your device and in your personal iCloud account. Your precious memories of your furry friend are yours alone.

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Your dog photos are stored locally on your device using iOS's standard storage mechanisms
  • iCloud Backup: If enabled, your data is backed up to iCloud following Apple's security standards
  • No External Servers: We do not operate any servers or databases that store your information
  • Your Privacy: Your dog's photos remain completely private to you

Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information or your dog photos with any third parties because we do not collect or have access to this information. The only sharing that occurs is:

  • When you choose to export photos to your device's photo library
  • Through iCloud sync between your own devices

Your Rights and Controls

You have complete control over your data in DoggyDaily:

  • Delete Data: You can delete any photo or all data by deleting the app
  • Export Photos: You can export your daily dog photos to your device's photo library at any time
  • iCloud Control: You can enable or disable iCloud backup for DoggyDaily in your device settings
  • Data Portability: All your dog photos can be exported to your photo library

Children's Privacy

DoggyDaily does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app can be safely used by dog lovers of all ages to capture memories of their pets.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Third-Party Services

DoggyDaily uses the following Apple services:

  • iCloud: For optional backup and sync functionality (controlled by you)
  • Photos Framework: For exporting your dog photos to your photo library

These services are governed by Apple's Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/privacy/

Data Retention

Your dog photos and data remain on your device and in your iCloud account until you choose to delete them. Deleting DoggyDaily will remove all locally stored data. To remove iCloud backups, you can manage them through your device's iCloud settings.
